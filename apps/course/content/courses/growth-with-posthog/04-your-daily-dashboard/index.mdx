## Words you'll meet in this chapter

- **North Star metric** — the single number that best captures the value your product delivers to users (nights booked, messages sent) — the one you'd optimise if you could pick only one.
- **Aha event** — the first action where a new user actually feels the product's value; the moment onboarding exists to reach.
- **Product-market fit (PMF)** — the point where a product has real, durable pull: some group of users keeps coming back instead of drifting away.
- **DAU/MAU** — daily active users divided by monthly active users, the share of a month's users who show up on a given day; a standard stickiness ratio.
