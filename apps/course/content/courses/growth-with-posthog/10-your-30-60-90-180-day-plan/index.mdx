> **In this chapter:** the course turned into a plan you can actually run — day 0
> commitments, then four phases (measure, keep, grow, compound) each with a goal, a
> checklist, exit criteria, and the anti-patterns that blow it up. The checklists and
> writing exercises below are interactive: everything you tick or write saves to
> [your workbook](/workbook) and is here when you come back.

**How this chapter works.** It's broken into eight short pieces — most are a one- to
three-minute read, and every checklist and writing box you touch saves to
[your workbook](/workbook). Two pieces carry an operator's video — watch it (or read the
transcript underneath), then read the short digest so the point sticks. Work through them
in order, or jump to what you need:

1. [How to use this plan](./01-how-to-use) — phases are gates, not dates; protect the order.
2. [Day 0 — commit](./02-day-0-commit) — one hour, no code: name the owner, agree the order.
3. [Days 1–30 — Measure](./03-days-1-30-measure) — see the truth about your funnel.
4. [Days 31–60 — Keep](./04-days-31-60-keep) — plug the biggest leaks before you spend on traffic.
5. [Days 61–90 — Grow](./05-days-61-90-grow) — open the tap into an audience you own.
6. [Days 91–180 — Compound](./06-days-91-180-compound) — turn the machine into a culture.
7. [What good looks like at each gate](./07-what-good-looks-like) — the four yes/no gate questions.
8. [Before you move on](./08-before-you-move-on) — commit to a start date, then the quiz.
