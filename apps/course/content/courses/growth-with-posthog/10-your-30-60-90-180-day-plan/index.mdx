> **In this chapter:** the course turned into a plan you can actually run — day 0
> commitments, then four phases (measure, keep, grow, compound) each with a goal, a
> checklist, exit criteria, and the anti-patterns that blow it up. The checklists and
> writing exercises below are interactive: everything you tick or write saves to
> [your workbook](/workbook) and is here when you come back.

**How this chapter works.** It's broken into fourteen short pieces — most are a one- to
three-minute read, and every checklist, calculator, and writing box you touch saves to
[your workbook](/workbook). A few pieces carry an operator's video — watch it (or read the
transcript underneath), then read the short digest so the point sticks. Work through them
in order, or jump to what you need:

1. [How to use this plan](./01-how-to-use.mdx) — phases are gates, not dates; protect the order.
2. [Which version are you on?](./10-which-plan-are-you-on.mdx) — the same plan, weighted by your stage.
3. [Who's running this](./11-role-based-plans.mdx) — how it bends to a founder, a cofounder, a first hire, a marketer.
4. [The zero-to-one version](./09-zero-to-one.mdx) — the pre-product-market-fit shape, if you have no users yet.
5. [Day 0 — commit](./02-day-0-commit.mdx) — one hour, no code: name the owner, agree the order.
6. [Days 1–30 — Measure](./03-days-1-30-measure.mdx) — see the truth about your funnel.
7. [Days 31–60 — Keep](./04-days-31-60-keep.mdx) — plug the biggest leaks before you spend on traffic.
8. [Days 61–90 — Grow](./05-days-61-90-grow.mdx) — open the tap into an audience you own.
9. [Days 91–180 — Compound](./06-days-91-180-compound.mdx) — turn the machine into a culture.
10. [The weekly growth review](./12-the-weekly-review.mdx) — the 30-minute ritual that keeps the plan alive.
11. [The experiment doc](./13-the-experiment-doc.mdx) — a reusable template, and how to score what to run next.
12. [What good looks like at each gate](./07-what-good-looks-like.mdx) — the four yes/no gate questions.
13. [After 180 days](./14-after-180-days.mdx) — the second channel, the first hire, defending the flywheel.
14. [Before you move on](./08-before-you-move-on.mdx) — commit to a start date, then the quiz.
