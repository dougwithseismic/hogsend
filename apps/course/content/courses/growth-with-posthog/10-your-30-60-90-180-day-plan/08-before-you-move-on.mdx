Three things are worth doing while the plan is fresh, before the week swallows it:

1. **Day 0 itself.** It's an hour with the whole team, and everything else in the plan
   assumes it happened. The [Day 0 checklist](./02-day-0-commit.mdx) keeps your place —
   tick things off as they land and they'll be here when you come back.
2. **The four gate reviews, in the calendar** — day 30, 60, 90, 180 from today. A
   30-minute review against each phase's exit criteria, with that phase's checklist —
   [Measure](./03-days-1-30-measure.mdx), [Keep](./04-days-31-60-keep.mdx),
   [Grow](./05-days-61-90-grow.mdx), [Compound](./06-days-91-180-compound.mdx) — open in
   front of you. Gates that exist in the calendar get held; gates that exist only in a
   chapter don't.
3. **A look at [your workbook](/workbook)** — everything you've written across the
   course (activation sentence, North Star, tracking plan, hypotheses, these
   checklists) in one place, each entry linked back to the chapter that produced it.
   That page *is* your growth plan now.

And if you're happy to share it — where are you starting from?
