**Words you'll meet in this chapter:**

- **AARRR** — the five stages a growth funnel breaks into: Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, Revenue.
- **Cohort** — a group of users bucketed by a shared trait, usually signup week, and tracked over time.
- **LTV (lifetime value)** — the total revenue one customer brings over the whole relationship, not just the first payment.
- **UTM parameters** — the `utm_source`/`utm_campaign` tags appended to a link to record where a click came from.
- **CDP (customer data platform)** — a pipe like Segment or RudderStack that captures events once and fans them out to many tools.
- **Holdout** — a slice of users deliberately kept out of a test to serve as a clean, untouched baseline.
- **PII (personally identifiable information)** — data that names a real person: emails, full names, and the like.
- **PHI / HIPAA** — protected health information, governed by HIPAA, the US health-data privacy law.
