Three things worth doing while this chapter is fresh:

1. **The weekly growth review** — booking it now, 30 minutes recurring with the
   operating-cadence agenda, is probably the highest-leverage calendar invite in the
   course. A rhythm that exists in the calendar survives; one that exists as an
   intention doesn't.
2. **The experiment backlog** — one shared doc, the hypothesis format from the
   experimentation program, ICE scores. Your dashboard's three worst numbers are the
   natural first entries.
3. **Chapter 10** — the full 30/60/90/180-day plan with interactive checklists. It's
   where all of this becomes a sequence with dates on it.
