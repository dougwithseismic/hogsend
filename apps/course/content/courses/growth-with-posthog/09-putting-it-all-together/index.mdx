**Words you'll meet in this chapter**

- **AARRR** — the five funnel stages: acquisition, activation, retention, referral, revenue.
- **North Star** — the single metric that best captures the value your product delivers.
- **Activation** — a new user's first real taste of value, pinned to a specific event. The **activation event** is that event; the **activation sentence** names it and its window ("activated = a project created within seven days of signup").
- **Identity graph** — the linked record of who a person is across email, Discord, Telegram and their anonymous first visit.
- **Lifecycle journey** — an automated message sequence (welcome, nudge, win-back) triggered by what a user does.
- **Lifecycle chart** — PostHog's view of how many users are new, returning, dormant or resurrecting each period.
- **Dunning** — the automated recovery of failed subscription payments.
- **Holdout / global holdout** — a slice of users deliberately left out of a journey (or every journey) so you can measure its real lift.
- **CAC** — customer-acquisition cost, what you pay to win one customer; **blended CAC** averages it across every channel.
- **Pixel + CAPI** — Meta's browser tracking snippet plus its server-side Conversions API; run together and deduplicated, they count conversions the Pixel alone misses.
- **EMQ** — Event Match Quality, Meta's score for how well your conversion data resolves to a real person.
