## Go deeper

- [Cal Newport — So Good They Can't Ignore You](https://calnewport.com) — the "career capital" argument this chapter's title borrows: build rare skills first, trade them for control over your work.
- [Reforge](https://www.reforge.com) — the fullest map of growth as a repeatable discipline, and of how growth-marketing, growth-PM, and growth-engineering roles differ in practice.
- [Andrew Chen](https://andrewchen.com) — growth as a cross-functional discipline ("Growth Hacker is the new VP of Marketing"), and a living example of an owned audience compounding into a career.
- [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com) — practical guides on growth and product careers, interviewing, and case studies; the reference most operators point new candidates to.
- [First Round Review](https://review.firstround.com) — operator-written pieces on founder-led growth and early growth hiring, useful for calibrating what a first-90-days plan should show.
