You can't install a mindset, but there are three things worth settling before you
touch a line of code — each takes minutes, and each saves real money later:

1. **Settle who owns growth** — one person accountable for measure → keep → grow. On a
   solo team that's you; writing it down still helps, because a hat is easier to drop
   than to wear.
2. **Try writing what "activated" means for your product** in one sentence — the
   moment a new user first gets real value. Chapter 2 will make it precise; what
   matters now is committing to the concept.
3. **Talk the order through with your team** — retention before acquisition. It's the
   single most valuable sentence to get everyone nodding at before any money goes into
   ads, because it's the one instinct that pulls the other way.

Then, when you're ready: Chapter 1 is the tool you'll measure with, and why measuring
is the job at all.
