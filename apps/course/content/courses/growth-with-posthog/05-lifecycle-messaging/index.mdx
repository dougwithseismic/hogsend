**Words you'll meet in this chapter.**

- **AARRR** — Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, Revenue: the five stages a user moves through, from first touch to paying and referring others.
- **Activation** — the moment a new user first reaches the product's core value; the target of onboarding.
- **Aha moment** — the first time the product's value clicks for a user.
- **Dunning** — the sequence of retries and reminder emails that recovers a failed subscription payment.
- **Holdout** — a randomly withheld control group, used to measure what a message actually *caused* rather than what merely correlated.
- **Incrementality** — the lift you can credit to a message, proven by comparing the messaged group against a holdout.
- **MRR** — monthly recurring revenue, the predictable subscription income a business earns each month.
- **ROAS** — return on ad spend, revenue divided by what you paid to acquire it.
- **CTA** — call to action: the single thing a message asks the reader to do.
- **DX** — developer experience: how pleasant a tool is to build on.
