---
title: "The full funnel, assembled"
description: "The complete path from a cold ad click to a referral loop, where every stage captures an identity edge. The feedback loops that make it a flywheel rather than a funnel, with the identity graph at the hub."
---

Run the two halves of this course's growth engine end to end — the ads and landing pages
that bring cold traffic in, and the linking and referral mechanics that keep it — and the
same thing happens at every stage: an identity edge gets captured. The path runs from a
cold ad click (tagged with an `fbclid`, the click id an ad platform appends, and UTM
parameters, the campaign tags you set, so you can trace where the visitor came from)
through email capture and account linking to a referral loop. At each step, something is
captured:

| Stage | Goal | What's captured |
|---|---|---|
| **Ad / content** | Cold reach | `fbclid` / UTM on the landing page |
| **Landing** | Intent | anon id stitched to the click |
| **Email capture + verify** | Own the contact | **email** (consented) |
| **Discord/Telegram link (perk)** | Densify the graph | **email ↔ Discord/Telegram** |
| **Lifecycle journeys** | Activate & retain | engagement events on your own spine |
| **Referral loop** | Compound | referrer ↔ referee edge, often a Discord role |

Read top to bottom it's a funnel; the point is what each row leaves behind — an edge you
keep.

What turns that funnel into a flywheel is that the outputs feed back to the top. The
[growth-loops model](https://www.reforge.com/blog/growth-loops) introduced in the
leaky-bucket chapter finally has all its pieces. The verified, consented emails you
captured become **custom audiences** (your own contacts uploaded so you can target
exactly those people) and **lookalike seeds** (that same list handed to the ad platform
to find new strangers who resemble them), so the next round of ads is cheaper and better
matched. The deeper conversions you report back straight from your servers — an
activation or a purchase, not just the click, and beyond the reach of ad-blockers — make
the platform's optimisation smarter. The owned channels you built make reactivation
near-free instead of another ad buy. And the referral loop lowers your **blended CAC**
(customer-acquisition cost averaged across every channel) a little more with every turn.

The identity graph sits at the hub. Every turn of the loop adds edges, and every edge
makes the next turn cheaper. That's the difference between a funnel you keep refilling and
a loop that compounds.
