> **In this chapter:** the AARRR framework and what each stage really means (with an
> example per stage); why "fix retention before scaling acquisition" — the leaky bucket;
> how to find *your* activation metric the right way, worked end-to-end; how to choose a
> North Star; and the other frameworks (growth loops, HEART, the PMF survey) and when to
> reach for them. This is the *what and why* of measuring — Chapter 3 is the *how*.

sticks. Work through them in order, or jump to what you need:
