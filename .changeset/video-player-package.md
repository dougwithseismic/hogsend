New package: analytics-first video player — normalized `video.*` watch-depth events across YouTube, Vimeo, and native HTML5, with a pluggable vendor-neutral emitter (Hogsend, PostHog, GA4, Segment, or custom), full player-state access (`getState`, `on("*")`, React hooks), and an open `ProviderAdapter` contract for custom sources.
